# UI Templates
A collection of UI Templates I am designing to showcase my ideas.

## The Process
I have been on the mission of designing UI Templates out of my ideas. Most of these templates don't do anything special but just show the core attractive part of it. In this repo;

### Anitube Home Page
I have designed a home page of an app named Anitube which is an app ment for anime lovers where they can watch fan mades of their favorite animes, enjoy mixes, meet studios and find more of their creations, meet large communities and upload their own fan-made content freely. The idea came about when scrolling for UI Templates on Pinterest. I fell on this image and decided to reproduce it myself using my tech stack. If you have luck and find that image back on pinterest or somewhere, you will see how it's very similar to this template.

### Visual Magazine
I have designed an interactive visual magazine which allows users to read articles on their favorite topics, meet authors, scroll catalogues all these using a fine UI. The Idea came out after scrolling my Mom's lock screen on her phone. It showed captivating images with a title below and a little description as "Discover how..". Each image and its title was for a particular article and navigating between them was smooth. I wanted to reproduce same but desktop-like.

## Languages Used
- `Html`
- `CSS`
- `Vanilla JS`

## Running the Templates
To see how the templates look like, simply
- Clone the repository
- In the templates folder, open `@index.html` in your browser

Once the template is open, click on any icon or button you see. Some open hidden content.
